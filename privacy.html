    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: October 2025

    Welcome to Hereditas. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

    1. Information We Collect

    When you use Hereditas, we collect the following types of information:

    • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, password, and any profile details you choose to share.
    • Scan and Search Data: We store the monuments, artworks, and landmarks you scan or search for, so you can access your history later.
    • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your device's location to help identify nearby monuments and provide relevant information.
    • Images: When you scan an object, the image is sent to our servers for recognition and may be retained to improve our services.
    • Technical Data: We collect device type, operating system, IP address, and usage statistics to help us keep the app secure and improve performance.

    2. How We Use Your Information

    We use the information we collect to:

    • Provide and personalize the Hereditas experience.
    • Identify and describe monuments, artworks, and landmarks.
    • Improve recognition accuracy and app features.
    • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, or support.
    • Comply with legal obligations.

    3. Sharing of Information

    We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only with:

    • Service Providers: We use trusted partners to help deliver our services, including:
      • OpenAI (AI processing)
      • Supabase (database and authentication services)
      • Eleven Labs (voice generation services)
    • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect the safety and rights of our users.

    4. Data Retention

    • Account information and scan/search history are retained for as long as your account is active.
    • Images you upload are stored on our servers and may be retained to improve recognition.
    • You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Section 7).

    5. Children's Privacy

    Hereditas is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 16 without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

    6. International Data Transfers

    Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We take appropriate measures to protect your data in accordance with applicable law (GDPR in the EU, CCPA/CPRA in the US).

    7. Your Rights

    Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

    • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
    • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
    • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
    • Restriction: Request limitation of how we process your data.
    • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
    • Opt-Out: Under the CCPA/CPRA, you may opt out of the sale or sharing of your data (we do not sell data, but you still retain this right).

    To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@hereditas.dev.

    8. Security

    We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no system is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

    9. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new version in the app and updating the "Last updated" date above.

    10. Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

    Email: info@hereditas.dev

    Website: hereditas.dev
